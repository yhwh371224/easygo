Brisbane Airport Has Two Separate Terminals

BNE International and Domestic terminals are approximately 2km apart. Unlike many airports, they are not connected under one roof — knowing which terminal you're arriving at matters for your pickup. EasyGo tracks your flight and confirms the correct terminal before you land.

International BNE International Terminal

Handles all international flights including Qantas, Singapore Airlines, Cathay Pacific, Air New Zealand, and others. Located in the northern precinct near the Skygate shopping area.

  • ✅ After customs & baggage: proceed to Ground Transport area, Level 1
  • ✅ Your driver will SMS you their exact parking bay location
  • ✅ Parking fees included in your fixed price — no extras
Domestic BNE Domestic Terminal

Handles Qantas, Virgin Australia, Jetstar, Rex, Bonza and all domestic routes. Located in the southern precinct, approximately 2km from the International Terminal.

  • ✅ After baggage collection: exit via Ground Transport, Arrivals level
  • ✅ Driver sends exact parking bay via SMS on landing
  • ✅ Shorter wait times than international — usually under 15 minutes

Not sure which terminal? EasyGo auto-detects your terminal from your flight number — just select your flight details in the booking form and we handle the rest.
