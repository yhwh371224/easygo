Tullamarine Has 4 Terminals — We Know Every One

Melbourne Airport (MEL) spreads across four separate terminal buildings. Getting to the wrong one wastes time. Your EasyGo driver knows exactly which terminal to meet you at — and tracks your flight in real time so they're there when you land, not when your booked time says you should have landed.

T1
Qantas Domestic
All Qantas domestic flights including QantasLink. Ground Transport pickup via the covered pick-up road directly outside arrivals.
T2
International Terminal
All international arrivals — Singapore Airlines, Emirates, Cathay Pacific, Air New Zealand and more. Ground Transport is Level 1, exits A and B.
T3
Virgin Australia & Jetstar
Virgin domestic and Jetstar budget services. The busiest domestic terminal by passenger volume. Pickup via roadside Ground Transport lane.
T4
Regional & Other Carriers
Rex Airlines regional routes and other smaller carriers. Separate building — tell us your airline at booking and we'll be at the right spot.
🚆
There is no airport train to Melbourne CBD. Unlike Sydney's Airport Link or Brisbane's Airtrain, Tullamarine has no rail connection to the city — only buses, rideshares, and private transfers.
