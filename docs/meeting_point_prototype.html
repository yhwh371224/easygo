MEL · Melbourne Airport

Airport Meeting Point

Select your terminal below to find where your EasyGo driver will be waiting.

Arrivals are on Level 2. After clearing customs, follow the signs to the Arrivals Hall — your driver will be waiting with a name board.

1

Hall A — General Arrivals (Flights AA, QF, EK)

Exit through the automatic sliding doors from Hall A. Your driver will be standing near the yellow bollards, holding a name board. This is the most common pickup spot.

2

Hall B — Asian Carriers (CX, MH, SQ, JL)

Hall B exits on the far right side of Level 2. Turn right after passing customs, follow the signs. Driver waits at the meeting pillar marked "B".

3

Overflow — Late-night / Charter Arrivals

For late-night charters or overflow arrivals, the driver will wait at the Ground Level kerbside (Level 1) near Door 7. Your reminder email will confirm which applies to your flight.

1

Kerbside — Main Exit

Exit through the automatic doors directly opposite baggage carousel 2–4. Your driver will be waiting on the kerbside with a name board. Do not walk to the multi-storey car park.

1

Main Exit — Virgin Australia Gate

After collecting baggage, walk through the main exit doors directly facing the taxi rank. Your driver waits immediately to the right of the exit, past the queue barrier.

Rex Airlines — Side Exit (Gate 3)

Rex flights use a separate baggage carousel and exit near Gate 3 on the south side. Follow the "Ground Transport" signs. Driver waits at the covered waiting bay outside Gate 3.
