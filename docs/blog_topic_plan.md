1. Title tag:   Keep under 60 chars, include focus keyword near the front
2. Meta desc:   Under 160 chars, include keyword + clear benefit + CTA
3. H2 tags:     3–5 per post, use related keywords naturally
4. Word count:  800–1200 words is the sweet spot for this niche
5. Internal link: Every post links to /booking/ or /sydney-airport-transfer/
6. Image alt:   Use keyword-rich alt text on every image
