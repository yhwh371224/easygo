MEL · Melbourne Airport

Airport Meeting Point

Select your terminal below to find where your EasyGo driver will be waiting.

Arrivals are on Level 2. After clearing customs, follow the signs to the Arrivals Hall — your driver will be waiting with a name board.

1

Hall A — General Arrivals

Exit through the sliding doors from Hall A. Driver stands near the yellow bollards with a name board. Covers flights QF, EK, AA and most full-service carriers.

2

Hall B — Asian Carriers (CX, MH, SQ, JL)

Hall B exits on the far right of Level 2. Turn right after customs and follow the signs. Driver waits at the pillar marked "B".

3

Overflow — Late-night & Charter Flights

For late-night charters or overflow arrivals, driver waits at Ground Level kerbside (Level 1) near Door 7. Your reminder email will confirm which spot applies.
